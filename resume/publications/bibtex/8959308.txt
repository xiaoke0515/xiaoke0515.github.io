  abstract={We present temporal quadratic solitons in doubly resonant temporal dispersive optical parametric oscillators without a walk-off in numerical simulations. In frequency domain, these cavity solitons correspond to coherent dual-combs with smooth envelopes in half-harmonic and pump fields. We also investigate the soliton existence region, and the soliton behavior with parameters in this cavity system. We believe our work offers valuable insights into the practical generation of quadratic soliton combs in optical parametric oscillators.},
